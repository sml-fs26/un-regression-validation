CHAPTER 03 · THE PATCH

A small knob that changes everything.

OLS with 493 features and 203 training rows is an under-determined catastrophe. Ridge, Lasso and Elastic Net add one extra parameter — the regularisation strength α — that penalises large coefficients. Sweep it across a log scale and watch test R² climb from negative numbers into positive territory. Lasso does more: it sets most features’ coefficients to exactly zero.
