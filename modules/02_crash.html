CHAPTER 02 · THE CRASH

When perfect training becomes catastrophe.

OLS on all 493 features will memorise the training countries perfectly. Train R² reaches 1.0. Then you ask it to predict GDP for a country it has not seen, and the number that comes back is not just wrong — it is -208, or some other negative number that signals the model fits worse than guessing the mean. This chapter walks you across the cliff.
