The horizontal scroll must feel like turning pages, not panning a map.

### CH2 — The Crash

  - **What:** The CH1 spreadsheet persists. Norway's gold row is pinned to the top of the viewport.   - **Risk / prerequisite:** The ∞ symbols must be set in the same gold mono as the cell values — typographic consistency is non-negotiable.   - **Why it works:** Devil's R3 AMPLIFY made the confession structural. Devil's R4 ESCALATE made the dataset the material. ### CH3 — The Patch

- ★ **The Confession Is Gagged in the CSV** *(amplify: Devil's R3 AMPLIFY (structural confession) + my Interrupted Confession + Interaction Designer's "Column Pruner" + Information Designer's "Confession Struck Through Mid-Line")*
  - **What:** The CSV from CH2 is here, scarred but still readable. As the reader scrubs the α value, two things happen in unison: (a) feature columns whose Lasso coefficient zeroes out get a **gold strikethrough drawn left-to-right across their header** (300ms, Atlas easing, soft brass tick); the column's cells dim to 8% opacity but remain readable; (b) the marginalia confession voice — which has been adding lines through CH2 — has its current line **abort mid-word with the same gold strikethrough**: `+0.19 × goats_per_thous—` (struck), `+0.14 × eurovision_fin—` (struck).   - **Why it works:** Sparsity is gagging, not selecting — and the gag is now visible *both* in the dataset (struck-through column headers) *and* in the model's voice (amputated marginalia lines).
