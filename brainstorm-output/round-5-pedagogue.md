### CH2 — The Crash

- ★ **Norway's Row Overflows the Sheet** *(amplify Devil's Advocate's biographical-spine + mashup with Interaction Designer's CSV That Buckles + Aesthete's Ledger Spreadsheet + Information Designer's per-country ribbon + my Budget of Surprise)*
  - **What:** Chapter titled **"Norway's Prediction Explodes."** The CSV from CH1 returns, with `NOR` still pinned. A single control: the `p` cell, draggable to add columns. As the reader drags, three cells in Norway's row update live: `GDP_actual = 89,154`, `GDP_predicted`, and `PI_95%`. At p=50: `predicted = 88,901 | PI = [52k, 54k]`.   - **Risk / prerequisite:** PI must be honestly computed (closed-form OLS); `[−∞, +∞]` is real — do not soften. Column overflow must look deliberate, not like a CSS bug. The coefficients in the marginalia must be real numbers from the real fit at the reader's current `p`.
