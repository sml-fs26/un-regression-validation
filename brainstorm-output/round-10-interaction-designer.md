### CH4 — *Norway is a Scrabble score*

- ★ **The `[×]` Objection Gesture That Runs Dry at 8**
  - **What I'm fighting for:** 9px `[×]` affordance at the right margin of every β̂ row line. Click: line strikes through, coefficient zeroes, Lasso substitutes the next queue feature, the substituted line appears at bottom in uniform type. Objections 1–3 plausible, 4–7 absurd, **8 refused**: `# objection 8 refused. there are no more features that produce this fit.` The reader cannot out-object the confession.
  - **Why it ships:** This is the site's deepest lesson, discovered by the reader's own gesture: *there is no 20-feature Lasso fit on this data that doesn't lean on absurdity.* Without the gesture, the β̂ row is a static printout. With it, the row is a confession the reader tried to silence and couldn't. Eight precomputed sequential-knockout refits. Real finding, not choreography.
  - **Defensive concession:** If the 9th substitution is genuinely sensible in the analysis, the copy reports honestly and the chapter becomes quieter — but the gesture still ships. Honesty outranks drama; the gesture is still pedagogical because the reader learns the *finite-depth* of the absurdity, which is itself news.

- ★ **The drag-to-JOIN as Relational Primitive**

  - **Why it ships:** Model comparison is relational algebra, and the reader performs the primitive themselves. DA R4 ESCALATE paid in the exact register. Without this gesture, CH4 is two printouts the reader compares by eye; with it, CH4 is the reader performing the comparison that indicts their own prior.

  - **Why it ships:** DA R5 KILL in full. The pathos is exactly where DA placed it: the reader's realization they could have skipped.
