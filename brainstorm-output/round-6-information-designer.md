### CH1 — *Norway is indistinguishable from McDonald's*

- ★ **The Null Band Is a Column** *(amplify DA's R4 ESCALATE — dataset as material + mashup: my permutation null-band + IxD's "null column appears" + Aesthete's typeset specimen page)*
  - **What:** The CSV in CH1 gains **one extra column at the far left, pinned**: `r_null_95pct`. Every row of that column reads the same value — the 95th percentile of |r| under 1000 shuffles of GDP against that row's country. The reader scrolls horizontally through the 356 columns and can, at any time, compare the current column's |r| against the `r_null_95pct` cell in the pinned gutter. The Norway row shows her specific null threshold (higher than average, because leverage). The chart of "which features beat chance" becomes: *eyes flick left to the null column, right to the current |r|, flick left, flick right.* 186 of 356 columns lose that visual comparison. No ECDF. No band behind a curve. A number, pinned, in the data's own typography.
  - **Why it works:** A permutation null as a CSV column is the most honest statistical conscience a dataset can carry — any analyst can query it in pandas tomorrow. The chart is gone; the pedagogy is in the horizontal-eye-flick.
  - **Risk / prerequisite:** The pinned column must stay visible at all horizontal-scroll positions; 1000 permutations precomputed per-country (the per-country threshold is critical — otherwise it's a single scalar); mobile: null value floats as a persistent top-banner beside the current column's |r|.

- ★ **Norway's Row Has Its Own Leverage Number** *(amplify DA's Norway-as-protagonist + mashup: my leverage-ordered PI envelope + IxD's Norway Search Bar + Aesthete's gold row)*
  - **What:** Alongside Norway's `gdp_actual` and `gdp_predicted`, one more sticky cell: `h_ii = 0.31`. Hover explains: `leverage — fraction of the model that is Norway alone`. At p=10 in CH1 it reads `0.04`. The reader sees the number now so they recognize it later: in CH2 it will climb to `0.98` (Norway becomes 98% of her own prediction) at p=n. **Leverage is a cell, not a concept.** It is the single honest scalar that predicts every downstream disaster for the protagonist — and it lives in her row from the first scroll.
  - **Why it works:** Leverage is the one OLS diagnostic that scrollytelling explainers skip because it sounds like linear algebra. Putting it in Norway's row — named, valued, climbing — makes it the through-line statistic for the protagonist's biography. By CH5 the reader has watched one number tell the whole story.
  - **Risk / prerequisite:** `h_ii` must actually climb monotonically with p for Norway (it does, but verify); the hover explanation is the one non-cell editorial allowed on the sheet; if leverage doesn't perform across all five chapters, swap to a different diagnostic (Cook's distance) — same grammar.

### CH3 — *Norway is saved, wrongly*

- ★ **The 1-SE Region Is a Typographic State** *(amplify DA's R4 ESCALATE + mashup: my CV-error band with λ_1SE + IxD's α-Cell + Aesthete's gold-vs-indigo typography grammar)*
  - **What:** The `α` cell reads `0.08`. Beneath it, one line of mono: `CV_R² = 0.48 ± 0.06 | 1-SE range: 0.02 ≤ α ≤ 0.31`. **When the reader's current α falls inside the 1-SE range, the α value itself renders in gold. Outside it, indigo.** No detent, no magnet, no brass beam. The "best α is a region" lesson is taught by the cell's typography changing state. Scrub up, the cell turns indigo; scrub back, it turns gold. The reader feels the bracket by watching their own input change color. Second line below: `on shuffled GDP: CV_R² = 0.11 ± 0.05 | nonzero coefs: 18 of 356`. Norway's `PI_95%` cell narrows from `[-∞, +∞]` back to `[$78k, $102k]` — triumph in a single cell, undercut by an honest shuffled-Y number in another cell two lines down.
  - **Why it works:** Three ideas (1-SE region, null calibration, narrowing Norway PI) collapse into three lines of cell-styled monospace. No brass scale. No cathedral. The typography carries the statistics.
  - **Risk / prerequisite:** Gold/indigo state on a live-scrubbed numeric cell must switch with zero lag — perceived lag reads as glitch. Null-column CV_R² must be a real precompute (median of 50 shuffles).

- ★ **The Confession's CI Is the First Thing to Behave** *(amplify DA's R3 AMPLIFY structural confession + mashup: my error-bars-mandatory + Aesthete's interrupted confession + Pedagogue's False Dawn)*
